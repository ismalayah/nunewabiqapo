Jhene Aiko aka Jhene Aiko Efuru Chilombo is an American singer and songwriter. As of 2022, Jhene Aiko’s net worth is $6 million. She accumulated her net worth through her singing and songwriting career. She is mainly known for her popular albums titled Souled Out in the year 2014 and Trip in the year 2017.

She has also released many popular singles and albums throughout her career and established herself as one of the popular and renowned singers in the American music industry. Many of her songs and albums top the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Jhene Aiko Wiki/Biography

Born on 16 March 1988, Jhene Aiko’s age is 34 years as of 2022. She was brought up and raised in an upper-middle-class family from Los Angeles, California, United States. Later, she moved to Slauson Avenue near Windsor Hills, Baldwin Hills, and Ladera Heights along with her family as their home burned down when she was just seven years old.

She completed her early schooling through homeschooling since high school and after that, she left her studies and took vocal lessons from Culver City, California. She also had to quit her vocal lessons as she got pregnant unexpectedly at the age of 20 years.

Career

Jhene Aiko started her career as a singer and musician in the year 2002 when she contributed vocals to a popular American boy band named B2K releases and sang a song in the adventure-comedy film titled The Master of Disguise. She was managed by Chris Stokes and signed a deal with The Ultimate Group, at that time people recognized her as the cousin of popular rapper Lil Fizz, but later it was revealed that she is not related to him and that was only a promotional technique used by her labels.

She has also been featured in track five of the popular album titled B2K The Remixes Volume 1 by R&B group and recorded the cover song titled Santa Baby. She has also appeared in the album titled Santa Hooked Me Up and appeared as a guest vocalist in the song titled Tease of the popular album titled Pandemonium.

After that, she has appeared in many popular music videos and soundtracks like Take It Slow, Uh Huh, Why I Love You, Same OI Same OI, Blow Ya Whistle, Barbershop, The Master of Disguise, The Proud Family, and others. In the year 2002, she has released a video for her single titled NO LOVE, after which she decided to release her debut album titled My Name is Jhene in the year 2003 but the album never got released due to some internal reasons.

After a short break in the year 2007, she made her return into the music industry and in the year 2011, she released a mixtape titled Sailing Souls on her official website named JheneAiko.com. To promote her mixtape she has released videos of her two singles titled My Mine and Stranger.

In the year 2012, Jhene Aiko was signed by Artium Records and she released the track titled 3:16 AM from the extended play titled Sail Out which was available online on iTunes. Later in the same year, she has performed the opening act on the concert tour titled Life is Good/Black Rage along with popular artists like Nas and Lauryn Hill. In the year 2013, she has been featured in the song titled Beware by Big Sean and performed on the concert tour titled Would You Like a Tour by Drake.

In the same year, she has released her extended play titled Sail Out and the very next year the third single titled The Worst from this EP was released. Later in the year 2014, she has performed the song From Time on the popular American television show Saturday Night Live. In the same year, she has released singles like My Afternoon Dream and To Love & Die as a part of her album titled Souled Out. After the release of her album, she has also released two other singles from the same album titled The Pressure and Spotless Mind.
