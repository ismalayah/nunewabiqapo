Toni Braxton Avoids Answering Questions About Wedding Birdman After Posting About Tour

May 08, 2024
blog

Toni Braxton got engaged to rapper Birdman last year but it seems her mind is all on her business.

The singer uploaded a photo to Instagram telling fans she was “Using my @unclebuds_hemp getting my body ready for THE TOUR!” The singer teamed with the company in September to promote its line of all-natural, hemp-infused skincare, personal care and pain relief products.

(@tonibraxton/Instagram)

Braxton is set to kick off her As Long As I Live Tour next year, which will include SWV as special guests. The show is launching in Columbia, S.C. at the Township Auditorium on Jan. 19 and wrapping up March 3 in Los Angeles’ Microsoft Theater.

Several fans shared their excitement about seeing the “Seven Whole Days” singer on the road.

“@tonibraxtonnn omg I can’t wait to see what her wardrobe for the tour looks like either. I already know she’s gonna look gorgeous as always 😩😍”

“It’s workin, an you’re ready, have a Great Tour, hope to see you in the Ville😄”

But others wondered about her impending nuptials to Birdman. The last major update fans got about it was during the last season of “Braxton Family Values.”

“What about the wedding 👰”

“WHEN’S THE WEDDING?”

“Hey beautiful so..when’s the wedding???❤️❤️❤️❤️💍”

Braxton got engaged to Birdman in September 2017 and after avoiding confirming the news, she made things clear in a teaser for BFV released back in February. But by the time the show returned to airwaves in April, fans and her family learned she and her fiancé would not wed in August as previously expected.

“B and I got engaged seven months ago, but we haven’t set a date because of my schedule,” she said on the show. “I hate that. You’re supposed to set a date and stick to it. But unfortunately, in our business, it can be challenging sometimes.”
