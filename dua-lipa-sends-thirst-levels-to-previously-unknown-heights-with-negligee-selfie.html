Dua Lipa sends thirst levels to previously unknown heights with negligee selfie

Is it getting hot in here, or is it just Dua Lipa? With winter storms impacting much of the United States, it’s safe to say that it is — in fact — just Dua Lipa as if there was any doubt at all.

Looking like a million bucks in a sexy black negligee, Dua Lipa knows how to work a camera, a room, and millions of Instagram followers, one of whom is Louisville native Jack Harlow, who wrote a song inspired by her simply because she’s so iconic — and totally stunning.

Stunning almost seems too broad a word to describe the lacy number Dua Lipa rocked for a GCDS show honoring the stylish and creative Giuliano Calza during Milan Fashion Week. And the comment section of Dua Lipa’s post shows that we’re not the only ones left speechless by her look. With everything from heart-eye emojis to several flames and responses calling her “unreal,” it’s hard to deny the effect she has on all of us.

In fact, you can call us Blanche Devereaux because we’re spritzing ourselves with a spray bottle of water and fanning ourselves with this one. We gave you a fair warning!

If you need a moment to pick your jaws up off the floor, you’re getting no judgment from us. Dua Lipa is just the epitome of perfection with this one, especially as she nails the brooding stare in the third shot. With simple makeup to allow the negligee to speak for itself, she did pair the ensemble with a bright red nail. In addition to a few perfect photos of herself, Dua Lipa also shared a video of Calza on the runway after his creations took the spotlight, and the backdrop for the show looks like it set up for a stellar night.

Now, if you’re a fan of Dua Lipa already, you know a smoke show Instagram post is nothing she’s unfamiliar with. When you’re as gorgeous as Dua Lipa is, everything you post ignites a wave of thirst worldwide. If you think about it, she’s doing the world an excellent service, keeping us hydrated as we reach for ice-cold water after scrolling through her Instagram account.

From crop tops, open-back dresses, and stylish suit jackets, Dua Lipa’s fashion sense is as stunning as the rest of her, and we’re just feeling really lucky to be here. Here’s to Dua Lipa, the now iconic black negligee, and Jack Harlow, who is undoubtedly singing his own tune, “Dua Lipa,” in his head all day after seeing that one.
