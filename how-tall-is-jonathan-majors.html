How tall is Jonathan Majors?

Jonathan Majors‘ name has been floating around a lot recently.

At first, this was due to his abrupt rise to full-blown star status. He was already a known name in plenty of circles, but Majors’ casting in the MCU as Kang the Conqueror, its next major villain — as well as his appearance in Creed III — rocketed Majors to superstardom, only for the actor to quickly tank.

Accusations of assault and a history of violence have been pouring in for more than a month now, leaving Majors’ future in the MCU unclear. While none of the accusations have been proven just yet, more people have reportedly come forward with further allegations of abuse, and most people predict Majors’ time in the massive franchise is at an end. That creates a wealth of issues for Marvel to traverse, and has kept the 33-year-old actor’s name in the headlines for weeks on end. As people continue to digest still-emerging details about the actor, they’re finding themselves with questions about everything from his past and future projects to his age and height.

How tall is the MCU’s Jonathan Majors?

In his appearances as Kang and Damian “Diamond Dame” Anderson, Majors looks to be pretty tall. He’s an inherently imposing figure as Kang the Conqueror, of course, and he entered the franchise in a role that largely saw him stay seated. He rarely stood up during Loki‘s run, which left many viewers to see him at his full height for the first time in Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania.

Majors stands at an impressive, but not daunting, 6 feet tall. His height is reported with a few variations by sites like Sportskeeda, CelebHeights, and IMDb, and all agree that he is between 5 foot 11 inches and a full 6 feet tall. He’s even said as much in the past, placing his height at 6 feet exactly, and this is more than enough confirmation for most people.

He certainly seems to have a few inches on fellow Quantumania star Paul Rudd, who is clearly shorter than Majors in shots of the pair together. Stills of their characters from the third Ant-Man film seemingly confirm that Majors stands right around the 6 foot mark, maybe even a bit taller if broad reports that Rudd is 5’11” are to be believed.
